Projects in detail

Project 1

InternationalMC

Minecraft plugin development (Feb 17 – Oct 4)

Project description

InternationalMC was a Minecraft plugin development project that initially focused on a single plugin called Valentines. As development continued, additional plugins were created and released under the same project.

On April 20, 2025, the Valentines plugin reached its peak, gaining approximately 2,000 downloads across four platforms (Paper Hangar, Modrinth, SpigotMC, and GitHub). This was the highest point of the project’s popularity. Downloads from later versions are not counted here, as the plugin was completely redesigned in the following months, effectively making it a new iteration.

Between May and August, the project became unstable. Development slowed due to holidays, frequent website redesigns, and shifting priorities—some changes later proved unnecessary and affected consistency.

After encountering issues and a ban on SpigotMC, all plugins were gradually moved first to Modrinth and Hangar, and eventually to Modrinth only.

Most plugins under InternationalMC were later removed due to incompatibility with newer Minecraft versions and personal time limitations.

The Valentines plugin remains available, although updates are currently infrequent due to a lack of new ideas for further development.
